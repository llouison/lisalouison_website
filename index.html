About Me

I've always been aware of how important it is to ask the right questions and how much an environment has an affect on people. As a corporate Interior Designer I built schools and offices around the world, helping to make people more productive in the places they spent the most time. I realized that a large part of that time is spent interacting with a screen, and so I made the pivot to Web Development to translate my skills from the built environment to the web.
